    DuckyDevCo

    The Ducky Dev Collective is a community-driven project aimed at fostering collaboration and support among developers and people in tech, particularly those who are remote students or face challenges connecting with others in the industry.

    The core objectives of this initiative are:

    Rubber Ducky Debugging

    Creating a supportive environment where developers can come together to act as "rubber ducky debugging" partners for each other, helping one another solve complex coding problems and learn more effectively.

    Inclusivity

    Establishing an inclusive and welcoming space for developers of all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals, to connect, share experiences, and enhance their skills in a friendly and non-judgmental environment.
